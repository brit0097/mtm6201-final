My Journey

My career path has been anything but conventional. After graduating from Law School, I quickly realized that the profession wasn't for me. The creativity that had always fueled my passions felt stifled, and I knew I needed a change.

From a young age, I was always drawn to creative outlets like reading, drawing, and experimenting in the kitchen. My culinary adventures have always been a source of joy, allowing me to explore new flavors and create unique dishes. This creativity, combined with my love for puzzles and logical challenges, led me to discover my passion for coding.

The thrill of solving complex problems and creating innovative digital experiences resonated deeply with me, making me pursue Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College.

My journey into web development has been fulfilling and adventurous. I'm excited to continue this journey, exploring new technologies, creating beautiful digital experiences, and sharing my neophilia along the way. Thank you for joining me on this path.
